True Colors™ was created to celebrate our individual differences, with an easy language that enables people to share in a positive self- and mutual understanding. While it begins with the understanding of self first and foremost, True Colors™ concerns improving one's relationships with others, based on the support of everyone's unique contribution.

The colors Green, Blue, Orange and Gold are used as an engaging metaphor to express the four sets of inborn character traits described within Temperament Theory. The colors express universal traits found within nature :

  • Green represents the natural life of earth-plants, signifying growth, complexity, and expansion
  • Blue represents the oceans and the sky-signifying depth, flowing movement, and association with "the heavens" (spirituality)
  • Orange represents fire-signifying excitement, action, and spontaneity
  • Gold represents the earth's vital resources-signifying solidity, value, and protection

"True Colors 24" is a natural expansion of True Colors™. While True Colors™ identifies four Types (according to the four Colors), or twelve Types (people often refer to themselves by their first two Colors), True Colors 24 evolves the system to consider each Color in order, in each person's True Colors™ Spectrum. The understanding of the influence of every aspect of one’s personality (each Color, in one’s Spectrum), helps people to understand the complexities of their personalities, further.
